## Purpose

This module records the public archive entry point for the `Translation Master Framework`.

The Translation Master Framework emerged from the 21-language publication line of `System and Freedom`.

Its role inside OathAI is method-layer evidence for cross-language structural continuity:

- source meaning preservation
- terminology governance
- emotional temperature calibration
- cultural adaptation
- cross-language alignment
- human-AI multilingual review
